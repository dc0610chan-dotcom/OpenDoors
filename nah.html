OpenDoors

This website is meant to support high school students who are still unsure about what career path they may want to pursue in university and beyond. Instead of guessing blindly, users can take an interactive quiz that helps them reflect on what they are interested in, where they naturally do well, and how they are doing academically.

By bringing together interests, strengths, and academic habits, the quiz is designed to guide users toward career directions that feel more realistic, personal, and worth exploring further. The goal is not to force one perfect answer, but to help students understand themselves better and feel more confident about their next steps.

Our mission is to help students explore possible career paths

Our team realized that choosing a career can be difficult, especially in Grade 12 when students are preparing to enter post-secondary education. Many people struggle to balance what they enjoy with what they want to pursue as a career. That’s why we created OpenDoors — a tool designed to help students, or anyone exploring a new path, discover careers that align with their interests and personality. OpenDoors provides suggestions to help guide users toward careers that may best fit them.

Back-End Logic

Hi! I’m Mikaela, a first-year Computer Science student with a minor in Business Administration at Laurier University. While figuring out my post-secondary path, I bounced between majors —psychology, engineering, English, chemistry— trying to find my fit. No online test or in-school guidance really helped me, and everyone seemed to have their own vision for my future, not mine. That’s why I believe a platform like OpenDoor is so important: a space to explore yourself, connect with others, and discover the many paths that exist. OpenDoor isn’t a solution to a question only you can answer—it’s the key that lets you explore the doors only you can walk through.
